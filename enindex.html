Personal introduction

Prof. Zhao received his bachelor, master and Ph.D. degrees from Shandong University (SDU), Technishe Universitaet Berlin (TUB), and Technical University of Denmark (DTU) in 2001, 2010, and 2014, respectively. He got the national scholarship for outstanding overseas students in 2014. He successively worked in State Grid Corporation of China (SGCC, China), Younicos AG (Germany), DIgSILENT GmbH (Germany) and Center for Electric Power and Energy (CEE) of DTU. He was selected among Qilu Young Scholar. In October 2017, he joined Shandong University as professor and doctoral supervisor.

He is senior member of IEEE, member of CIGRE C6.C1.33 (Integrated Energy system) Working Group and CIGRE C4.56 （Electromagnetic transient simulation models for large power grid with large-scale power electronics connected）Working Group, member of Chinese Society for Electrical Engineering (CSEE) and Vice Chair of Shandong Energy Research Association. Currently he is an Associate Editor of the IET Journal of Engineering and Youth Editor of Power System Protection and Control. He is also the associate editor of Special Issue of IEEE.Transactions on Energy Conversion, Editor-in-chief of International Journal of Electrical Power& Energy Systems.

Prof. Zhao has been exploring in wind power generation and its grid connection and application of energy storage system in power system for a long time, making a series of original achievements. He has been invited to deliver reports on international conferences and seminars for many times and won the Applied Energy 2018 Best Paper. In addition, he participated in several EU and Nordic energy projects, developed several power system software tools and owned several EU patents.
